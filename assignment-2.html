Queering the Map: an Interview with Lucas Larochelle. Sissy Screens.

Interview

Objective

Conduct two one-hour interviews with stakeholders of a socially engaged software.

Description

For the second assignment, you will research the history and relevance of a socially engaged software, and reach out to two stakeholders affliated with the software to conduct two one-hour interviews. The interviews are first recorded in audio or video then transcribed to text. You will compile and edit your transcriptions – fixing grammatical errors and adding visuals, hyperlinks, and citations to provide the full context of the interviews for your readers.

Elements to include in your case study:

Title
  • Created a title for your interviews.
Date & Location
  • Specify when & where the interviews were held.
Introduction
  • Write an introduction & summary for the software.
  • Who are you? What drew you to researching this software?
Questions
  • This is the most important part of this assignment. Craft your questions carefully and be as specific as possible. Return to the elements for the Case Study assignment to get some ideas. The more you're able to research the software beforehand, the more nuanced your questions may be.
Bibliography
  • Citations in MLA style

You will be sharing your interviews with your classmates on 3/18. You will be graded based on the articulateness of your questions and the effectiveness of your interview outcomes.
