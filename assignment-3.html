Research Paper

Objective

Write a 8~10 page research paper on one or more socially engaged software.

Description

For your last assignment, you will dive into a deep research on one or more socially engaged software. As starting point, identify a broad and reoccuring theme within the software discourse (see possible themes below). Consider how commerical and preditorial software exerts dominance and erasure, as well as how the socially engaged software of your choice resists such control. Formulate a 8~10 page paper in your own words (bibliography excluded).

Elements to include in your paper:

Title
  • Who are your readers? Consider how your title draw them in.
Themes
  • What are the central themes in your paper? Why are you researching them?
Thesis Question
  • Narrow down your inquiry. What is the main question you're hoping to answer through this paper?
Arguments and Evidence
  • Branching off from your thesis question, come up with a list of arguments
  • Backing up your arguments with evidence. Make sure that you're citing reliable sources
Bibliography
  • Citations in MLA style

You will be sharing your interviews with your classmates on 3/18. You will be graded based on the articulateness of your questions and the effectiveness of your interview outcomes.
